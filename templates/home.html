When Are African Traditional Clothes Worn?

Lobola Negotiations

During lobola negotiations, families gather to discuss marriage arrangements. Traditional attire is worn to show respect, dignity, and cultural pride.

Weddings

African weddings are vibrant and colourful celebrations. Couples and guests wear beautiful traditional outfits such as Ankara, Shweshwe, or Kente.

Imigidi (Celebrations)

Imigidi are traditional celebrations commonly practiced by Xhosa people in South Africa. They celebrate the return of young men from initiation school (ulwaluko).

Ukuthwasa

Ukuthwasa is a spiritual journey to become a traditional healer. Special clothing reflects connection with ancestors.

General Celebrations

Traditional clothing is worn at graduations, family events, and celebrations.
